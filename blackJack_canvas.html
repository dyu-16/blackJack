Blackjack!

How to Play

Press Deal to start a new game.

Player is dealt an up card, followed by a down card to the dealer. Player is then dealt a second up card, followed by an up card for the dealer.

If a player is dealt two cards of the same rank, they can Split the cards and choose to play each of them separately, putting up a bet for each one.

Aces can have a value of 1 or 11, automatically set depending on current Total Hand Value.

Dealer and Player Bust when they have Total Hand(s) Values of over 21.

Press Hit to be dealt another a card.

Press Stand to stop asking for cards.
